# AI Testing Strategy

## Overview

An AI testing strategy defines how an organization plans, prioritizes, and governs validation activities across data, models, applications, and operations.

## Core Elements

- Business objectives and risk alignment
- Scope across models, pipelines, and user-facing systems
- Test environments, datasets, and tooling
- Entry criteria, exit criteria, and release gates

## Strategic Priorities

- Focus testing depth on high-risk use cases.
- Combine functional, safety, performance, and compliance checks.
- Establish ownership across engineering, data, QA, and governance teams.
- Review strategy regularly as models, prompts, and regulations evolve.
