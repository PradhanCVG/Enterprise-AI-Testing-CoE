# AI Testing Standards

## Overview

AI testing standards define the minimum expectations for consistency, traceability, and quality across enterprise AI initiatives.

## Standard Areas

- Test case design and review criteria
- Dataset quality and lineage requirements
- Model evaluation metrics and thresholds
- Documentation, approvals, and audit evidence

## Recommended Controls

- Use standard templates for test plans and results.
- Require versioning for prompts, datasets, and models.
- Define mandatory regression checks before release.
- Maintain evidence for compliance, risk, and production readiness.
