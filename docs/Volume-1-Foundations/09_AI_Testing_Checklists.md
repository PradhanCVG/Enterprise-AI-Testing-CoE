# AI Testing Checklists

## Overview

Checklists help teams perform repeatable reviews before model promotion, deployment, and production monitoring.

## Example Checklist Categories

- Requirements and risk review
- Data quality and privacy validation
- Model accuracy, robustness, and fairness checks
- Security, observability, and rollback readiness

## Usage Guidance

- Tailor checklists by use case criticality.
- Use them at design, test, release, and post-release stages.
- Track sign-off ownership and unresolved issues.
- Treat checklists as living assets, not one-time documents.
